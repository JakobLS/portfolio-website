Some projects I've worked on

CancerSEEK - Cancer Detection Through Multi-Analyte Blood Test

Thrive Earlier Detection launched early 2019 following a $110 Million Series A financing. Learning about the company and the research caught my interest. After reading their publications I thought I would have a fair chance of improving their cancer detection model on the published proteomics and genomics data.

Maintaining specificity at >99% I managed to improve sensitivity for Breast, Colorectal and Pancreas cancers by 100%, 31% and 21%, respectively. These three cancer types stand for more than 4 million new cases annually.

Although one of my initial goals with the project was to attempt a collaboration with the company upon completion, they had moved their focus to other approaches they deemed more promising for improving sensitivity. Nevertheless, I gained deep understanding of the entire Machine Learning workflow as well as insights into product/market fit as a result of the project.

Assessing Abnormal Chromosomes using Machine Learning

Complementary on above with the purpose of improving specificity by evaluating abnormal number of chromosomes in the blood. Specificity was indeed improved to 99.8%, slightly higher than before.

The project gave me further extensive knowledge in the entire EDA process, among those data transformation and feature engineering, as well as model stacking, model tuning and unbiased model evaluation.

Finding Habitable Exoplanets with CNNs

Analysed light emitted from distant stars using Deep Learning techniques where all pre-processing and model architecture steps were developed by me. The data was transformed into spectra and fed into Convolutionl Neural Networks to predict three target variables; temperature, gravity and metallicity.

The long-term goal of the project was to find habitable exoplanets and this new method decreased the error on earlier Machine Learning approaches by more than 35 times. Consequently, I developed an extensive understanding of the entire computer vision workflow.

Predicting Stock Prices

As my graduation from Engineering was approaching, the core motivation for this project was to create an automated stock-trading system for the Stockholm Stock Exchange to help pay back the loans I had so willingly accumulated during my time at University.

Although it turned out to be a more difficult task than anticipated I learned a great deal about applying deep learning techniques on time series data.
